Chicken Soup

Description

Chicken soup is a warm, comforting dish enjoyed across cultures for its soothing taste and nourishing qualities. Made by simmering chicken with vegetables, herbs, and spices, it is both flavorful and wholesome. Often called a “comfort food,” chicken soup is especially popular during cold weather or when someone is feeling unwell, as it is light, easy to digest, and refreshing. Its rich aroma and balanced nutrition make it a favorite home-cooked meal, bringing a sense of warmth, care, and homely comfort with every spoonful.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil all Ingredients
  2. Wait till desired consistency is achieved
