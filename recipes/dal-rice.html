Dal Rice

Home

Description

Dal rice is a simple, comforting dish that holds a special place in everyday meals across South Asia. Made by cooking lentils until soft and lightly spiced, then serving them over steamed rice, it's nourishing, filling, and easy on the stomach. The dal can vary from yellow moong or toor to masoor, each bringing its own texture and flavor, while the rice provides a neutral, fluffy base that balances the spices perfectly.

What makes dal rice so loved is its warmth and versatility. It can be kept plain for a soothing, home-style meal or elevated with ghee, cumin tempering, pickles, or a squeeze of lemon. Whether eaten during a busy weekday lunch or as comfort food on a quiet evening, dal rice represents simplicity, nourishment, and the taste of home.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Toor Daal
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt
  • Asafetida

Recipe

  1. Wash Dal and Rice in water separately
  2. Add water to Dal until completely submerged in water
  3. Add Turmeric powder and Asafetida to Dal
  4. Add twice the amount of water to the rice as the rice
  5. Steam both in Nutri Pot
