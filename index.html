Dal rice is a simple, comforting dish that holds a special place in everyday meals across South Asia. What makes dal rice so loved is its warmth and versatility. It can be kept plain for a soothing, home-style meal or elevated with ghee, cumin tempering, pickles, or a squeeze of lemon.
